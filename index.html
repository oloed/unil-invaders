How to play:

Press 'a' and 'd' to move the canon, 'm' to shoot and 'b' to summon Aebischerzilla, the protector of the EPFL (you can do this only once!) .

Backstory:

In this small game you are the last hope to save your beloved Rovex Learning Center© from invaders coming from the distant UNIL galaxy
